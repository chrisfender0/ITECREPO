Byte and Router are Terrific Recyclers

 An amusing picture book featuring popular characters Byte the Bear and Router the Raccoon. After writing a school report on pollution, Byte and Router start a successful recycling club. This book entertains kids while teaching them about how to reduce, reuse and recycle.

Wire the Weasel’s Circle of Friends Learn about Social Networks

An interesting and informative book that can benefit children and parents. Wire the Weasel learns from mistakes when he shares information online that should be private. Wire becomes suspicious and feels uncomfortable when he receives a threat from another player in an online game. Fortunately, Wire has the courage to share his feelings with his friends and family. This book offers tips to protect kids from online bullying and teaches them how to act as they socialize online.
